Membership Requirements

Learn about the qualifications and expectations for joining our honor society

Academic Requirements

Minimum Qualifications:
  • Overall GPA of 3.5 or higher
  • Grade of A- or better in any CS course taken
  • Currently enrolled in or completed at least one computer science course
  • Demonstrated interest in technology and programming
Preferred Qualifications:
  • AP Computer Science A or AP Computer Science Principles
  • Personal programming projects or portfolio
  • Participation in technology-related activities
  • Leadership experience or community service

Expectations & Commitments

Member Responsibilities:
  • Attend monthly general meetings
  • Participate in at least 2 workshops per semester
  • Contribute to one society project annually
  • Complete 10 hours of community service
  • Maintain academic standards (3.5+ GPA)
Benefits Provided:
  • Honor society cord for graduation
  • Official transcript notation
  • Leadership opportunities
  • Networking events and mentorship
  • Scholarship recommendations
